Assignment 6; due Week 8.

Requirements

Create a comment form with JavaScript validation. Make sure it fulfills these requirements:

  • The user is presented with an empty comment form with a field for their name, email address and comment.
  • When the form is submitted, the form is validated.
  • Validate that the name field is not empty.
  • Validate that the email address field is not empty, and has at least one "@" and one "." in it.
  • Validate that the comment field is not empty.
  • If the form is invalid, the fields that are invalid should be highlighted in some way.
  • If the form is valid, the page should alert() that your comment was successfully saved.
